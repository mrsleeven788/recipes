Avgolemono Soup

Ingredients:

Chicken Broth
3 eggs
lemon juice
angelhair pasta
cubed chicken breast
salt
pepper

Directions:

1) Defrost chick and boil.  Cut into cubes.
2) Bring chicken broth to light boil.
3) Add angelhair pasta and chicken breast.
4) In separate container crack eggs and whisk.  Add some lemon juice to container.
5) Stir egg mixture continuously while very slowly ladling chicken broth into the mixture.  Ladle broth until satisfied that egg mixture has fully merged without shocking the eggs.
6) Pour egg mixture back into chicken broth and stir.
7) Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
